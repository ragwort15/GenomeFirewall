Honest-explanation note (how to read "evidence")

Evidence category (i) means a known resistance gene or DNA change was detected (mechanistic). Category (ii) means the models found only a statistical association — a feature-importance / SHAP value does not prove a biological cause. Category (iii) means no known resistance signal was found (absence of markers is weaker evidence than mechanism, and is gated on the drug's molecular target being present).
